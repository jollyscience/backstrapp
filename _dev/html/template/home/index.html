What is Homeowner Support?

Homeowner Support provides answers to a very common question: What can I do if I'm having trouble paying my mortgage? There are options, and the sooner you take action the better. This site will help you:

Educate yourself on some of your options

Learn about some of the common mortgage options available to homeowners.

Get advice from fellow homeowners

You're not alone. Join the forums to ask questions and learn from homeowners who know what you're going through. If you've been struggling with your mortgage, share your story, answer questions and help others learn from your experience. You can also follow the latest in mortgage topics and advice in the Homeowner Support Blog.

You've got options.

Here are some options that may work for your situation. Your mortgage company can help determine exactly what you're eligible for.

Home Affordable Unemployment Program (UP)

An option for unemployed homeowners that can temporarily reduce or suspend their mortgage payments. More

Traditional Refinance

Switching to a lower or fixed interest rate can make your mortgage more affordable. More

Home Affordable Refinance Program (HARP)

A possible option if the value of your home has dropped and your mortgage is owned by Fannie Mae or Freddie Mac. More

Deferment and Forbearance

May lower your mortgage payments so you can get back on your feet during a temporary financial hardship. More
